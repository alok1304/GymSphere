Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At GymSphere, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, and phone number when you register for an account or use our services.
  • Payment information when you make a purchase or subscribe to a membership.
  • Usage data, such as IP address, browser type, and device details when you access our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your membership, payments, and provide you with the requested services.
  • To send promotional offers, updates, and newsletters, if you have opted in.
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience based on usage data.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except for the following purposes:

  • To process payments through third-party payment processors.
  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to government requests.
  • To prevent fraud, enforce our terms of service, or protect our rights and property.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time, subject to legal restrictions.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

GymSphere reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. You will be notified of any significant changes through email or our website.
